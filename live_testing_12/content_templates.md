## Response Time Guidelines

- **Questions**: Respond within 2-4 hours if possible (shows you're helpful and active)
- **Showcases**: Within 24 hours (still feels timely)
- **Discussions**: Within 48 hours (can be more thoughtful)
- **Email**: 24-48 hours for first response

---

## Customization Checklist

Before sending any message, ensure you've:

- [ ] Personalized with their specific context
- [ ] Referenced something specific from their post/work
- [ ] Provided genuine value (answer, resource, insight)
- [ ] Kept it concise and scannable
- [ ] Checked for typos and clarity
- [ ] Only mentioned supyagent if truly relevant
- [ ] Added a question or call-to-action if appropriate
- [ ] Used their preferred tone (formal vs casual)

---

## Tone Guidelines by Platform

- **Reddit**: Casual, helpful, technical. Use markdown formatting.
- **Twitter**: Brief, punchy, emoji-friendly. Max 280 chars.
- **GitHub**: Professional, detailed, code-focused. Use markdown.
- **Email**: Professional but warm. Clear subject lines.
- **Discord**: Casual, friendly, quick. Use code blocks.
- **LinkedIn**: Professional, business-focused, results-oriented.
- **Hacker News**: Technical, honest, detail-oriented. No marketing speak.

---

## A/B Testing

Track which templates perform best:

1. **Engagement rate**: Replies, upvotes, likes
2. **Conversion rate**: Click-throughs to website
3. **Relationship building**: Follow-up conversations
4. **Tone reception**: Positive vs neutral vs negative responses

Iterate based on what resonates with your audience.
